Cut- To delete something you have typed.

  • highlight the text
  • click Cut

Paste- To put text in a new spot

  • highlight the text
  • click Cut
  • click the spot where you want to place the text
  • click Paste

Undo- Click Undo to reverse or cancel your last action. Each click reverses one more action.

Redo- Click Redo to reverse the action of the Undo command. Each click reverses one more Undo command.

Keyboard Keys:

Use Tab to indent at the beginning of a line.

Use Backspace to remove text to the left of the cursor.

Use Delete to remove text to the right of the cursor.
